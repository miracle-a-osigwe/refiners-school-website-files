Refiners Schools Mission, Vision and Values

Our Motto: Perfecting God's Will

SCHOOL MISSION

Our Mission is to nurture and foster with high moral values, discipline, and sound mind for learning in a conducive and enabling environment through creative and challenging learning experiences.

SCHOOL VISION

Our vision is to perfect the will of God in young stars by refining, nurturing, and moulding them for eminence and excellence in purity with the fear of God, as well as offering a robust, and moral education acceptable nationally and globally to the benefit of the society and mankind.

To accomplish our vision, we endeavor to:

  • Embrace our responsibilities as individuals.
  • Collaborate with parents and other stakeholders.
  • Demonstrate the highest standards.
  • Promote Tolerance, respect, trust, honesty and hard work.
  • Strategize progress and Celebrate success.
  • Recognise positive contributions.

SCHOOL CREED

I am a unique and intelligent Refiners Scholar

I am responsible, respectful and a creative thinker

Being refined for excellence

I am an ambassador of Refiners and an agent of change,

An instrument of influence

I am always ready for each school day

I will strive to be the best I can be

So, help me God

To walk in your perfect will

CORE VALUES

  • Excellence
  • Faith
  • Integrity
  • Nobility
  • Equity
  • Reliability
  • Service to others
